Anime: Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese anime based on the manga created by Masashi Kishimoto. The story follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition and dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village.

Throughout his journey, Naruto faces many challenges, builds strong friendships, and discovers the true meaning of strength and perseverance.

About Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan takes place in a world where humanity lives behind walls to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans.
